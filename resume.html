About me

I have always been interested in Social Sciences and Sciences. Within the years I have defined my set of skills through school, jobs, and projects. During my early years at my community college, I worked mainly in positions that gave my stronger soft skills while I was also learning them whilst also learning hard skills. One of the things I did and still volunteer for is as a Citizens Bond Oversight Committee (CBOC) member at the SJECCD, working with a team of people to monitor the use of bond money. I first learned this while also working as a senator with the Associated Student Government at Evergreen Valley College.

Once I left to further my education at San Jose State University (SJSU) I started to gain a large interest in programming and data analysis. Through this, I was still defining my skills through the people I was interacting with (i.e. Electrical Engineers, Communications Studies, Computer Science, Psychology) to name a few. Through working with them I was able to gain my soft skills to have a deeper understanding to communicate with different fields of education both personally and professionally.

Since COVID-19 I have been able to utilize my eagerness to learn new things to my advantage with learning IT Support and Creating this website. I am currently looking to find a company that can use my experience to their benefit and utilizes my understanding of how to work with people to get the job done.
