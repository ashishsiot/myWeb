About Me

Posted by Cory on September 9, 2020

I have always been interested in Social Sciences and Sciences. Within the years I have refined my skill set through school, jobs, and projects. During my early years at community college, I worked mainly in positions that strengthened my soft skills whilst learning new hard skills. One of the things I did and still volunteer for is the Citizens Bond Oversight Committee (CBOC) as a member at the San Jose Evergreen Community College District. I have been working with a team of people to monitor the use of bond money within the SJECCD. I first learned this while also working as a senator with the Associated Student Government at Evergreen Valley College.

I furthered my education at San Jose State University (SJSU) I started to gain a large interest in programming and data analysis. I was still refining my skills through the people I was interacting with (i.e. Electrical Engineers, Communications Studies, Computer Science, Psychology). By working with these different individuals, I was able to gain a deeper understanding on how to communicate with different fields of education both personally and professionally.

Since COVID-19 I have been able to utilize my eagerness to learn new things to my advantage with learning IT Support and Creating this website. I am currently looking to find a company that can use my experience for their benefit and could utilize my understanding of how to work with people to get the job done.
